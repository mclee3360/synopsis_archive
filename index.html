MAL Rewrite Synopses Archive

The MAL Rewrite team on MyAnimeList is an organization dedicated to creating orignal, high quality synopses for the various anime, manga, and other entries on the site. As a member of the team, I have contributed to a variety of different synopses as both a writer and an editor. This page is a record of all the different synopses I have been involved in.

For clarification, the writer is the one who creates the synopses originally. They also can choose whether or not to have a hand in the "final edit," where they look over the changes that the editors have made and make some finishing touches. The editors are the ones who clean the written synopses and make sure that the content is up to standard. Each synopsis goes through two phases of editing. All synopses are checked over by the coordinators for approval before upload, where they make some final edits of their own as well.

All synopses that have been written by MAL Rewrite are tagged as [Written by MAL Rewrite] at the end of the synopsis on the website.

Symbols:
* I took part in the final edits.
** The synopsis has not been uploaded onto the website yet.
